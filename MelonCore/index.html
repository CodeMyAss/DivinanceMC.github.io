    MelonCore is a Minecraft Bukkit plugin that offers essential commands.

    Commands/Permissions

    • /heal [player] - meloncore.heal
    • /feed [player] - meloncore.feed
    • /teleport [player] (/tp [player])- meloncore.teleport
    • /teleporthere [player] (/tphere [player]) - meloncore.teleporthere
    • /head
    • Global chat (/g)
    • Team chat (/t)
    • A currency for achieving certain tasks. (VenomRewards points)
    • A shop to spend your VenomRewards points (/shop)
    • Allows polls to be created by staff members
    • Allows players to vote for polls (/vote)
    • Alert staff by reporting players (/report)
    • Check which staff members are online (/staff)
    • Spectator mode
    • And much more...
