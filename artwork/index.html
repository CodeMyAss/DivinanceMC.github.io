Solitude Destroy the Core

Solitude is a dual core DTC map inspired by a medieval feel and parts of the city Solitude from Skyrim.


Special Features:

- A lot of stuff slowing down rushers

- Enchanting System, Bottles of Enchanting in almost every chest and enchanting tables in every house.

- Easter Eggs


This map is loaded into the Overcast Network Map Repository. Ask a staff member to /setnext it!
